Butter Chicken with Basmati Rice and Naan Bread

The best dinner kit that Gobble offers! Always a top scorer!

Butter chicken is one of India’s most beloved dishes, and we know you’ll love it, too!Sautéed pieces of diced chicken are folded into a rich butter sauce made with cream, crushed tomatoes, and Indian spices. It’s served alongside basmati rice, a refreshing cucumber and tomato salad with orange-cumin vinaigrette, and warm naan.

Ingredients

  • Diced chicken breast
  • Indian-style butter sauce
  • Basmati rice
  • Naan
  • Cilantro
  • Persian cucumber
  • Roma tomato
  • Orange-cumin vinaigrette

Supplies to gather

  • Nonstick saute pan (10 inches or larger)
  • Kitchen knife & cutting board
  • Salad bowl
  • Microwave-safe bowl
  • Kitchen tongs
  • Cooking spoon
  • Paper towels
  • Salt & pepper
  • Cooking oil

Did you know?

Our Indian-style butter sauce, traditionally known as makhani sauce, is an Indian-spiced tomato sauce that uses butter for a creamy flavor and texture. It is used as a base sauce to prepare popular dishes such as butter chicken and chicken tikka masala.

Cooking Instructions

  1. Let's get started: Gently rinse and pat dry your produce
  2. Step 2: Slice your persian cucumbers into thin rings. As thin as humanly possible
  3. Step 3: Deep fry your chicken until they turn black and your fire alarms go off
  4. Step 4: Spoon the cold Indian butter sauce atop the blackened chicken and thin sliced cukes
  5. Step 5: Let's Plate! Make it look bussin
